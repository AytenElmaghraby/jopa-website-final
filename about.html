ABOUT OUR BRAND

JOPA Dental is an Egyptian brand founded by Dr. Ahmed Ehab, a professional MMA athlete and dentist, dedicated to revolutionizing athletic protection.

At JOPA Dental, we believe that true protection empowers performance. We aren't just making products; we are crafting the confidence you need to excel in your arena. Every milestone we reach is a testament to the trust you place in us, and we are constantly evolving to ensure we deliver nothing less than excellence.

OUR IMPACT

In a short span of time, we have successfully provided high-quality custom mouthguards to over 500 athletes. Our gear has been field-tested and trusted by professionals and enthusiasts.
